About Love Joy


Lovejoy is an English indie rock band featuring lead vocalist Will Gold (Wilbur Soot),
guitarist Joe Goldsmith, bassist Ash Kabosu, and drummer Mark Boardman. They debuted
on May 10, 2021 with their EP Are You Alright? along with a music video for
their song One Day, directed by Gold. More about the band can be found here.
